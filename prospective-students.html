To Prospective Students:

I usually look for one or two self-motivated master students each year (starting in Fall) to work on interesting research in computer vision, such as objec detection, visual tracking (in 2D videos or multi-modal), image super-resolution, fine-grained image retrieval, person re-identification, adversarial attack and defense and other related areas. Qualified students will be supported by research assistantships. If you'd like to know my research work, please refer to my publications at this page.

If you are highly creative, have good grades/coding skill and interested in computer vision research, please do not hesitate to send your CV and transcripts of grades to my e-mail (davidzhang@zjnu.edu.cn).

Application deadlines:

  • Spring: September 15th

  • Fall: January 15th

Note: GRE could be WAIVED for excellent applicants.

In addition to master students, motivated undergraduate students within ZJNU are also welcome. If you're interested in conducting reseach with me, please contact me.
