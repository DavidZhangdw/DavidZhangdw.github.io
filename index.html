About Me (GitHub) (DBLP) (ResearchGate) (GoogleScholar)

I'm currently a Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Zhonglong Zheng in MLV Lab at College of Mathematics and Computer Science, Zhejiang Normal University, and Co-supervised by Prof. Xiaoqin Zhang at College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Wenzhou University. Before that, I received my B.E. degree in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Huaiyin Institute of Technology, Huaiyin, China in 2017. During the spring of 2021, I was working with Prof. Yanwei Fu as an intern at Institute of Science and Technology for Brain-inspired Intelligence (ISTBI), Fudan University.
My research interests include computer vision, deep learning and reinforcement learning.
